# The Problem Statement
Astronomy Enthusiasts have passion and curiosity but very limited avenues to learn, collaborate, and engage with peers who share similar interests.

## Features
**Learning Modules**  Structured, interactive modules designed to take users from the basics of space bodies to understanding complex deep-sky objects like the  Andromeda, Aries etc.

**Weekly Quest** Engaging weekly challenges that encourage users to learn more and collaborate with other people of similar interests and complete the given challenges.

**Curiosity Corner** A dedicated space where enthusiasts can ask questions, quench their curiosity thirst.

**Daily Wonder** A daily, astronomical fact with image, historical events occured on that date and any space body passing near by earth.

## Tech Stack
- Platform: iOS

- Language: Swift

- UI: Storyboards
